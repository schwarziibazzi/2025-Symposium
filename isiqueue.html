Mobile App

isiQueue: Electronic Queue Management System

By digitalizing the queue management process, the system improves customer service efficiency, reduces waiting times, and minimizes the frustrations associated with traditional queuing methods.

Project Overview

This work contributes to the broader digital transformation in Papua New Guinea by showcasing how smart, technology-driven solutions can modernize service delivery in key sectors. It highlights the potential of ICT to enhance convenience, streamline operations, and improve overall customer experience within service-based organizations.

Technologies Used

  • Android, HTML, CSS and JavaScript Frontend
  • Xampp, PHP and Firebase Backend
  • Firebase Database
  • Local Hosting
  • Data Visualization
  • Responsive Design

The Innovators

Graham Boko

Final Year MCS Student

Graham Boko, originally from Madang and West New Britain, developed an Electronic Queue Management System (EQMS) for the Bank of South Pacific (BSP) branch in Madang as part of his academic project.

The system enables customers to generate tickets and join queues virtually, while also allowing them to monitor their status via mobile phones and receive notifications as their turn approaches.

The project was motivated by Mr. Boko’s personal experiences and observations of prolonged queues and service delays at the BSP Madang branch.
